Travel Reviews

Emma's honest opinions about places she's visited

Ritz Paris Hotel

An absolutely stunning experience from start to finish. The service was impeccable, the rooms were luxurious, and the location couldn't be better. The concierge arranged everything perfectly for our stay.

Reviewed in April 2023

Argo Restaurant, Santorini

Incredible seafood with a view that's worth every penny. We had the grilled octopus and sea bass which were perfectly prepared. The sunset view from the terrace was breathtaking. Reservations are a must!

Reviewed in June 2023

Zermatt Ski Resort

World-class skiing with the most spectacular mountain views. The resort is well-organized with excellent lifts and a variety of runs for all skill levels. The aprs-ski scene is lively and fun too!

Reviewed in January 2023

Park Hyatt Tokyo

An iconic hotel with exceptional service and breathtaking views of Tokyo skyline. The New York Bar on the 52nd floor is a must-visit, especially in the evening when the jazz band plays.

Reviewed in October 2022
